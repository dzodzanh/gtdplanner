GTD Planner Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data

Last Updated: April 21, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our GTD Planner mobile application ("App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

GTD Planner is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Task and Project Data: The tasks, projects, and other productivity data you create within the App.
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device to ensure the App functions properly.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous information about how you use the App to help us improve it.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Improve and optimize the App
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Detect and prevent technical issues

Data Storage and Security

Your task and project data is now stored on the cloud. This enables syncing across multiple devices and improved backup. We take strong measures to ensure your data is secure and protected in the cloud environment.

Third-Party Services

Our App may use certain third-party services to help us analyze how our App is used and to improve functionality. These third parties may have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
