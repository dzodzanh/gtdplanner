About GTD Planner

GTD Planner is a productivity app designed to help you implement the Getting Things Done methodology in your daily life. Our app helps you capture, clarify, organize, and review your tasks so you can focus on taking action.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new task?

Tap the + button on the main screen and enter your task details. You can assign categories, due dates, and priority levels.

Can I sync across multiple devices?

Now GTD supports cloud-based tasks, syncing across multiple devices.

How do I add subtasks to a task?

Open a task, then tap the subtask button within the task view. This will allow you to create subtasks that are nested under the main task. You can organize and prioritize these subtasks as needed.

Is my data secure?

We take data security seriously. Your data is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to your task data.
